HoneyComb

Welcome to HoneyComb a web app to help you choose a movie when you can't. You can input a number of criteria and our program will create a list of the best movies for you! HoneyComb has 38 different genres to choose from and over 1000 movies between the years of 2020~2023 to be recommended!

Disclaimer! If the movie doesn't update that means there is no movie that fits your criteria.
